4. **2024 · Generative AI + Authoring.** Gen AI & LLM on Databricks, Weights & Biases LLM-engineering badges, demo-skill certifications, and the first book.
5. **2025 · Scale & Mastery.** The elite Azure Master Architect Program, NVIDIA DLI work (OpenUSD, edge AI, satellite imagery), Google Cloud AI — and the second book.
