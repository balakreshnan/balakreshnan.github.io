About

A pragmatic AI leader who ships outcomes

Two decades of bridging boardrooms and engineering rooms — turning emerging AI capabilities into measurable business value across manufacturing, energy, building tech, healthcare, and the public sector.

As Chief AI Officer (ProServ) and Chief Architect, I serve as an innovation evangelist — providing thought leadership and end-to-end solution architecture for ambitious enterprise programs. My focus spans Generative AI, Agentic AI, large-scale machine learning, Industrial IoT, and modern data platforms across Microsoft, open-source, and multi-cloud ecosystems.

I've helped global organizations stand up AI Centers of Excellence, deliver billion-dollar digital transformation deals, and operationalize hundreds of PoCs into production. I'm equally comfortable advising a CEO on AI strategy and architecting fine-tuning pipelines for 400B+ parameter models on H100 / H200 clusters.

I serve on the Industry Advisory Boards of UW Milwaukee's Connected Systems Institute and Purdue Indiana, and have contributed to NSF proposals for Manufacturing USA and the Wisconsin Technology Council.
